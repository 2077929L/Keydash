About

Keydash is an app with several game modes for improving typing speed/accuracy, eliminating typing errors whilst also being entertaining and fun. For each game mode, you can compare your skills with other users with global statistics as well as your own performance with personal statistics.


Targeted for a broad area of users, the main measurement for scoring is through WPM or words per minute which is a great way for hardcore gamers to compare their typing efficiency to improve their coordination and reaction time essential for all games, or the professional typist who wants to improve their typing efficiency in the workplace and wants a unique and fun way to do so.


There are 4 game modes which the user can choose from:

  • English Dictionary words: showing words from the english dictionary
  • Random alphanumeric: showing words not in the english dictionary and including numbers, e.g. ppprri190
  • Random alphanumeric + punctuation: showing words in the english dictionary and including numbers and punctuation, e.g. 1!;190al*
  • Sentences or paragraphs: showing full sentences or paragraphs.
